# 🏗️ Automation in Construction (AiC) - Elsevier Knowledge Base

## 💎 Journal Identity (Q1)
- **Publisher**: Elsevier
- **Focus**: IT throughout the life cycle of buildings and engineering structures (Design, Engineering, Construction, Management).
- **Impact**: High emphasis on novel methods, system interoperability, and practical construction technology.

## 📝 Manuscript Structure (Mandatory)
1. **Front Matter**: 
   - Title (Concise and informative).
   - Highlights (3 to 5 bullet points, max 85 characters each including spaces).
   - Abstract (Structured, strictly no references).
   - Keywords (Used for search engine discoverability).
2. **Main Content**:
   - 1. Introduction (Objectives and background).
   - 2. Material and Methods (The MAS framework: PydanticAI, LangGraph, etc).
   - 3. Results (Simulation data from cronograma.csv).
   - 4. Discussion (Implications for Civil Engineering).
   - 5. Conclusions.
3. **Back Matter**:
   - Data Availability Statement.
   - Acknowledgments.
   - References.

## 🔗 Citation Style: `elsarticle-num`
- Numerical style in square brackets (e.g., [1], [2]).
- List references in the order they appear in the text.
- DOI inclusion is highly recommended for all articles.

## 🤖 AI Declaration (PEIXOTO Guard)
- Generative AI must be disclosed in a separate "Declaration of Generative AI and AI-assisted technologies in the writing process" statement.
